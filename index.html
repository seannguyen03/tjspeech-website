Meet Our Captains!

Sean Nguyen

NON-INTERP CAPTAIN

Sean has competed in Extemp and Impromptu for the last two years at local, state, and national circuits. He has spearheaded the creation of a Speech website and merchandise. He hopes to grow the novice base and strengthen relationships between veteran speakers and incoming members.

Meet Our Coach!

Marie Bakke


TJ Speech is coached and sponsored by Marie Bakke. Ms. Bakke has coached Speech and Congressional Debate at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science & Technology since 2011. She has taken students to local tournaments, NCFL Grand Nationals, NSDA Nationals, and to invitational tournaments including Harvard, U-Penn, Columbia University, Wake Forest, George Mason Patriot Games, Emory University, and more. Ms. Bakke is an NSDA Diamond Coach, an active member of the National Speech and Debate Association, and president of the Northern Virginia Forensics League (NoVAFL). Her philosophy of coaching speech is that everyone can participate in speech, with a focus on the concept that excellence is the goal, winning the bonus. She is committed to making speech and debate a more diverse, equitable, and inclusive activity.
