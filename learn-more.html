SPEECH AND FORENSICS

- ABOUT THE TEAM -

TJ Speech is a community of communicators. We participate competitively in a wide variety of events ranging from dramatic interpretation to original oratory, improving our public speaking and oral skills. Whether you like acting, current events, or extemporizing, join TJ Speech to have fun and improve your communication skills!

Uniqueness is powerful.

TJ Speech wants you to be yourself. Our commitment to inclusion across race, gender, age, religion, identity, and experience drives us forward every day. Our diversity fuels our spirit and our success.
 
The speech community are leaders of intentional inclusion and are committed to providing its participants, judges, and coaches the opportunity to pursue excellence in their endeavors by assuring an atmosphere of mutual respect.


We Are Members Of...

​​​The National Catholic Forensic League

The National Catholic Forensic League is a major organization of high school speech and debate events along with the National Speech and Debate Association. TJ Forensics competes in the Washington-Arlington Catholic Forensic League at 5 WACFL tournaments over the course of the year to qualify students for the National Catholic Forensic League Grand National Tournament in May.




The Virginia High School League

The Virginia High School League hosts several tournaments every year including Regionals 5C and Super-Regionals, culminating in the State Championship Tournament in March. 



The National Speech and Debate Association

The National Speech and Debate Association is a student debate society that directs high school speech and debate events. Every June, the National Speech and Debate Association hosts the NSDA Finals, which students qualify for at the Virginia District Tournament in March.
